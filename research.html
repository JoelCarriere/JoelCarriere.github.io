Machine Learning Projects

One of my current main focuses in on using open-source algorithms and consumer-grade equipment to determine human wrist kinematics. Designing experiments, creating datasets, and refining model parameters has lead me to a high-degree of success in this. The video illustrates a 3D markerset generated through 2D videos and computer vision techniques.

I have successfully achieved a high-degree of accuracy when using both a multi-camera and single camera approach to solving this issue. Through the use of deep neural networks, I was able to achieve an angular value with a mean absolute error of 5 degrees when compared to the current gold-standard.
