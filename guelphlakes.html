Welcome to Guelph Lakes Golf and Country Club

We are a beautiful 18-hole golf course located in the rolling hills of the countryside. Our course is well-maintained and perfect for golfers of all skill levels. We also have a pro shop and a clubhouse with a restaurant and bar.
